FAQ

The minimum purchase is 30 units.

2.What are the payment methods?

  • MERCADO PAGO: You can pay with all enabled credit and debit cards, Pago Fácil, Rapipago or Mercado Pago.
  • BANK TRANSFER: You can safely transfer to our account with your bank.

3.What is the cost of shipping?

Shipping prices vary depending on the distance, weight and volume of your package.

4.Where can I receive my order?

Wherever you choose to receive it, whether it is your home, your workplace, a friend's home, the place you prefer or the post office branch of your choice.

5.How do I know that my order is ready?

When your order is ready to be picked up, we will send you an email.

6.How are our products made?

Our products are 100% handcrafted by specialized and trained professionals in this area.

7. What materials are our products made of?

They are made with natural materials of excellent quality, as we care about the health and well-being of our customers, while being environmentally friendly.
